SAE India - BIT Sindri

SAEINDIA BIT SINDRI provides the young budding talents an opportunity to design, develop, manufacture, and test vehicles that operate on land. We as a Team, Participate in two of the major events organized by SAEINDIA namely BAJA & SUPRA. We offer assistance to the fresher's to guide them in this field and equip them with adequate knowledge so that they can participate in various Technical events at Home Campus and elsewhere. In addition to that we also guide and encourage our students to participate in technical workshops, seminars, technical symposiums, various paper presentations, projects & ongoing research works in the field of the latest automotive technology.
